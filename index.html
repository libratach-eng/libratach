The reading crisis

America has a reading problem. We built something about it.

The National Assessment of Educational Progress found that more than half of American fourth graders cannot read at a proficient level. This isn't about intelligence — it's about how reading is being taught, and how little practice most kids get.

Libratach won't fix the whole system. But it will give your child a daily reading habit that builds real speed and real comprehension — one session at a time.
